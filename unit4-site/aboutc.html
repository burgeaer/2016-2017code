The Creators

James Clark

James Clark- One of leaders of Project Reality, was a programmer and developed his own coorporation which started Silcon Graphics. In his Early youth he was relbellious due to his parents divorce, but after he joined the navy and studied at the University of New Orleans. He started teaching, as well as becoming a extemely successful entrepreneur. He teamed up With Hiroshi using his knowledge Silcon Graphics in order program The N64.

Paraphrased from Biography.com

Hiroshi Yamauchi - The Ceo at Nintendo for many years until he died in 2014, He started working with them in 1947 after his grandfather passed on. He was critized for being too young and not qualified to work in this field. He proved them wrong though, In fact he ran the comapny like no else did. He introduced the making of toys and video games and releasing new technolgies into the world. When he was young he never thought he would become a Ceo of a comapny this big. He wanted to be an engineer, but the World War II took that away from him. He still ended up doing something in that field by working for Nintendo. His work inspired what is now possible today

Paraphrased from Arcade Sushi.com
