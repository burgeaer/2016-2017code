The Parts

The Parts that make up the Nintendo 64

    What Does What?
  • Power Pack - Provides power to the system
  • RCA Cables - Yellow(video) Red(audio) White(audio) Allows the video and audio to show up on your TV
  • Game Pack - Is the Game you want to play which goes in the top of the N64 system
  • The System - Allows you to play the Nintendo 64 games and processes them on to your TV
